Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Who We Are

All Wheels Tag & Title Services ("we," "us," or "our") is an MVA-authorized tag and title agency located at 201 N Frederick Ave, 2nd Floor, Gaithersburg, MD 20877. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at allwheelstagandtitle.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using our website:

  • Contact form submissions: Full name, phone number, service needed, and optional notes when you submit the "Get Started" form.
  • Chat interactions: Messages you send through our website chat agent.
  • Phone calls: When you call us using the click-to-call links on our site.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • General geographic location (city/region level, not exact address)
  • Referring website or search terms

This data is collected using cookies and is used solely to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. Google Analytics data is anonymized and we cannot identify individual visitors from it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your service inquiries and call you back
  • Process your tag, title, or registration requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate with you about your inquiry

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use PayMeGPT for form processing and chat services. Your form submissions are transmitted through their platform.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. Google may process data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for secure data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for Google Analytics to track site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly without them.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request to know what personal information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
